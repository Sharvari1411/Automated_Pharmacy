Description

Manna Health Mix is a natural health mix of whole grain cereals, millets, and pulses that promotes overall health, It is very nutritious and balanced with essential proteins, dietary fiber, and iron.

How to use

Add few spoons of this powder to water or milk and then cook it for 2-3 minutes. You can add salt, sugar or honey for taste. This can be then served and taken as a full meal, porridge or beverage. You can use this product two times a day. Do not use if after the expiry date.

Storage

Store it in a cool and dry place. Keep away from sunlight..
