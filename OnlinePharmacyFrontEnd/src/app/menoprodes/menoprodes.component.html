Description

Menopro Powder is a nutritional potent supplement that contains soy isoflavones, calcium, vitamin D, 30 essential vitamins, minerals, flavor (mango extract) and soy protein isolate used for managing the symptoms of menopause.

Indications

It is used to control menopausal symptoms like irritability, anxiety, depression, hot flushes, etc., postmenopausal osteoporosis, and hypercholesterolemia in menopausal women.

Directions of Use

Take one serving of powder in a glass of milk or lukewarm water, stir well until the powder dissolves. It can be taken once daily or use as directed by the physician.
