Description

Myovedic Oil is an ayurvedic medicinal oil with anti-inflammatory, anti-arthritic, and anti-rheumatic properties used for relieving the pain in joints and muscle. This oil is enriched with herbs and does not contain any chemicals or preservatives. Massaging with this oil on the problem area improves blood circulation, relaxes the muscles, reduces the inflammation, improves the joint mobility, reduces the joint and muscle stiffness, and reduces the pain.
