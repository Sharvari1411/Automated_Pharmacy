Description

My Dr. Pain Relief Oil is an ayurvedic herbal pain reliever oil used for back pain, neck pain, shoulder pain, knee, and joint pain. This oil is non-irritant, non-greasy, and non-allergic in nature.

Ingredients

Karpooram, Puteeha Satwa, Yavani Satwa, Bhutruna Tailam, Gandhapuro Patra Oil, Tila Oil, Coconut Oil, etc..

How to use

Apply a small amount of My Dr. Pain Relief Oil on the affected area and massage gently. Warm the area with a hot water bag. Do this 4 times a day.

•For external use only
